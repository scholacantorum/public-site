Fill your Monday nights with music and friends!
Fill your Monday nights this summer with music and friends! Join us for our annual summer sing-alongs of beloved choral masterworks. You bring the voice; we supply the scores and the accompaniment. Superb Bay Area conductors and pianists keep the music flowing.

Admission is $25 for each sing ($30 at the door), or you can purchase a FLEXPass that gives six entries for $125. Use it for one entry to each of the six sings, or six entries to one sing, or any mixture. (Students under 25 get free admission with ID.)
