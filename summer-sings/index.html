Fill your Monday nights with music and friends!
Join us this summer for our annual sing-alongs of beloved choral masterworks. You bring the voice; we supply the scores and the accompaniment; and a variety of local conductors stitch it all together.

Admission is $25 for each sing, or you can purchase a FLEXPass that gives six entries for $125. Use it for one entry to each of the six sings, or six entries to one sing, or any mixture. (Students under 25 get free admission with ID.)

7:30pm Mondays July 7 – August 11
